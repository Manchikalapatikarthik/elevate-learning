# Getting Started with ESP32 IoT Projects

ESP32 is one of the most powerful and affordable microcontrollers
used for IoT and embedded systems development.

## Why ESP32?

- WiFi + Bluetooth
- Low power consumption
- Dual-core processor
- Easy integration with sensors

## Applications

- Smart Home Systems
- Automation
- Robotics
- IoT Monitoring
- AI-integrated devices

## Why Engineering Students Should Learn ESP32

ESP32 helps students build practical real-world projects
using embedded systems, sensors, AI integration,
automation, and IoT technologies.

It is widely used in:
- Smart agriculture
- Robotics
- Home automation
- Industrial monitoring
- AI-powered embedded systems

## Conclusion

Learning ESP32 development gives engineering students
hands-on experience with modern embedded technologies
and helps in building strong project portfolios.
